Ploy on Google Cloud

Ploy is a cloud-first Identity Governance and Administration (IGA) platform that automates access governance across Google Cloud, SaaS, and collaborative environments. It helps organizations turn any type of access into temporary, time-bound, and fully auditable access while enforcing least-privilege and Zero Trust principles.

Built for Google Cloud customers Security · IAM · Compliance Cloud-native, multi-tenant SaaS
Overview

Ploy gives security, platform, and DevOps teams complete visibility into identities, roles, and permissions across Google Cloud projects and folders, as well as SaaS and internal systems. From onboarding to offboarding, the platform provides workflows that make access requests, approvals, and expirations simple, consistent, and audit-ready.

With thoughtfully designed automation, organizations reduce manual tickets, minimize access creep, and stay aligned with standards such as SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR.

How it works on Google Cloud

1. Centralized visibility and governance

Ploy connects to Google Cloud IAM, Google Cloud projects, and Google Workspace to discover users, service accounts, groups, and roles. It surfaces who has access to which resources, where privileges are elevated, and where access should be temporary.

2. Temporary, time-bound access

Engineers and operators request just-in-time access to Google Cloud resources. Ploy applies policy-based approvals, time limits, and automatic revocation so that elevated permissions exist only for as long as they are needed.

3. Integrated audit and compliance

Ploy leverages Cloud Audit Logs and Cloud Logging to record access activity and correlate it with approvals and expirations inside the platform. This creates a single source of truth for audits and incident investigations.

4. Data-driven insights

Access events and configuration data can be exported into BigQuery for deeper analytics, reporting, and integration with existing security operations or GRC tools.

Google Cloud products used
  • Identity and Access Management (IAM) – core policy and role management across projects and resources.
  • Cloud Identity / Google Workspace – identity provider for workforce identities, groups, and SSO.
  • Cloud Audit Logs & Cloud Logging – capture, correlate, and retain access events for compliance.
  • Cloud Resource Manager – consistent governance across organizations, folders, and projects.
  • BigQuery – advanced analytics on identity and access data where required.
Customer value
  • Reduce security risk by eliminating standing elevated access and enforcing least-privilege.
  • Shorten audit preparation with centralized, search-friendly records of who had access, when, and why.
  • Lower operational overhead by replacing ad-hoc access tickets with repeatable workflows.
  • Support modern Zero Trust strategies for Google Cloud-based environments.
